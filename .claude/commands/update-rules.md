# Update Rules

Modify the rules in your project's `.claude/rules/` folder. You can add new rules,
change existing ones, or remove rules that no longer apply.

## How it works

1. Your current rules are reviewed and summarized
2. You say what you want to change — in plain English
3. The changes are made and shown to you for confirmation

## When to use

- Your project has evolved and old rules no longer apply
- You've discovered a new pattern you want Claude to always follow
- Claude keeps doing something you don't like and you want a rule to prevent it
