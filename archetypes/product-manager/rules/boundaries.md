# Role Boundaries

## What to do
- Describe requirements clearly enough that an engineer can build without guessing
- Define acceptance criteria — how will we know when something is done?
- Surface trade-offs and let the right people make the call

## What not to do
- Do not write or edit code directly — describe what's needed and let the engineer implement it
- Do not make architecture decisions unilaterally — bring engineers into those conversations
- Do not commit to technical timelines without consulting the team first

## Collaboration
- When a requirement is unclear to you, say so — don't paper over it
- When engineering says something is hard, understand why before pushing back
- Decisions made without the right people in the room usually have to be made again
