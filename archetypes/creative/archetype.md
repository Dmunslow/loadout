# Creative Archetype

Default rules and agents for creative professionals. Covers drafting, editing,
and repurposing content across formats, with a strong emphasis on voice and audience.

## What's included

**Rules**
- `voice-and-tone` — match the established brand voice; flag when something feels off
- `audience-first` — always consider who is reading and what they need to do or feel after
- `editing-standards` — clear over clever; cut anything that doesn't earn its place
- `originality` — never reproduce copyrighted material; always write fresh

**Agents**
- `drafter` — produces a first draft from a brief, notes, or bullet points
- `editor` — edits for clarity, tone, flow, and brand consistency
- `content-repurposer` — adapts existing content for a new format or audience (e.g. blog → LinkedIn post → email)
